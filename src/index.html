In 2008, John Paul Tannous had a vision to introduce light beer to the market. With no experience, and limited knowledge in the field, John began with Coors Light, his favorite brew from university. In their first month they sold 50 cases of beer, had only 2,000 sq ft of warehousing, one part time employee and shared the resources of 3 company trucks.
