who we are

A pioneering sales and distribution organization serving Trinidad/Tobago and Caricom Nations.

TBC is a division of NWT Enterprises, a general merchandise distributor company. In 2015, NWT Enterprises, entered into a new era of growth in the beer business, and so the bevridge company was formed as a separate division dedicated to serving this growing trend.

We are a privately owned company located in Trinidad and Tobago. Our regional operations are made possible through strategic partnerships with distributors in the Caribbean.

In late 2009, our first brand introduced was Coors light, the first main imported player in the light beer segment. However, success did not come overnight. It was during this time that the company started to take shape. We developed the right tools, the right team, and the right approach.

Our competitive advantage has always been to spot upcoming trends and act quickly on them. Allowing us to position our brands well ahead of the competition and capitalize on the wave of the market.

Founded as a general merchandise distributor in the early 90’s named NWT Enterprises, we have grown and diversified. Today our operations span:

General merchandise


Fast Food Packaging


Real Estate Development


Beverages
